Krishnapatnam Restaurant

For the first time in Hyderabad - Andhra, Telangana & Rayalaseema delicacies under one roof !! Krishnapatnam Kitchen & Bar on Road No-10 Jubilee Hills aims to bring you the culinary & traditional legacies of Andhra, Telangana & Rayalaseema to your palate with amazing drinks. We aim to serve the original flavourful delicacies of 3 regions with authentic spices & ingredients straight from the Telugu heartland. For those who are in the mood to relish delicious local Telugu cuisine with an uptown traditional ambience, this will be their favourite go-to restaurant. While you feast on our flavourful delicacies, you can club it with delicious drinks of your choice.
